General Instructions

MCQ Quiz on Relations and Limit of Function

Full marks: 30
Avaiable time: 60 minutes

Please read the instructions carefully:

  1. The test contains total **32 questions** each having one mark. But you can get at max **30 marks**. There is no negative marking.
  2. Duration of the test is **60 minutes**. The countdown timer in the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time. The test will end automatically when the timer reaches zero.
  3. To answer a question, you just need to tap or click anywhere on the line where the correct option is present.
  4. You can click on a question number in the Question Palette, which looks like a blue rectangle with three horizontal white lines (see image below), to navigate to it directly.
  5. The questions you have "Answered and Marked for Review" will be considered for evaluation.
  6. Submit Quiz only when you have visited all the questions.
  7. After submission you can see your score and download (save as pdf) the question paper as a pdf.

Question Palette Symbols:

  • You have not visited the question yet or you skipped the question.
  • You have not answered the question but you marked for reviewing later.
  • You have answered and marked the question for review because you may have some doubt.
  • You have answered the question without any doubt.
  • It shows the question you are currently looking.

Buttons:

There are four buttons bottom to each question. They are as follows

  • This button helps you to go to previous question one by one.
  • This button helps you to clear selection after you select an option by mistake and you want to skip.
  • If you are unsure about a question and would like to return to it later, you may mark it for review.
  • This button helps you to go to next question one by one.
